About

My name is Oleksandr Kukharyk, and I’m the creative mind behind the studio. At the start of 2026, I established OK Design as my independent practice in Belgium, turning 15+ years of international experience in design into a dedicated B2B service.

I work directly with B2B clients worldwide who need senior-level creative execution without full-agency overhead - handling everything from initial concept to final delivery across every required touchpoint.

FAQ

01

What is required to initiate a project with OK Design?

Every project starts with a thorough analysis of your business goals and target audience. Once the strategic and visual direction is locked in, a custom structural blueprint is created to ensure a flawless final output.

02

How is the cooperation handled legally?

OK Design is an officially registered business entity based in Belgium (VAT/BTW registered). The studio operates under transparent B2B contracts and issues official invoices for local and international corporate clients.

03

What design fields does the studio cover?

OK Design handles the complete visual ecosystem: brand strategy, high-end UI/UX digital platforms, and premium 2D/3D motion graphics — eliminating the need to coordinate multiple separate vendors.

04

What is the payment structure?

The standard process involves a 50% advance invoice to secure the production slot, with the final 50% balanced upon project approval and final asset delivery.
