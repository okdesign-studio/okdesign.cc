GoodGame Cyberlounge

Client: GoodGame
Year: 2021
EXPLORE FULL CASE
01 / OVERVIEW

THE CHALLENGE

Develop a cohesive social media visual identity for a premium esports club. The scope included designing a 12-post grid concept, defining visual templates for content pillars, creating modular block-style Stories, and producing promo banners and motion graphics—all strictly aligned with the brand's bold cyberpunk aesthetic.

About the Brand

GOOD GAME is a next-level esports and relaxation space featuring 42 high-end PCs across 4 gaming zones, a PS5 Luxe Room with an 86" 4K screen, and a Michelin-trained brand chef menu. Designed for ultimate ergonomics, it offers wall-mounted monitors, unique legless tables, and custom hands-free hookah holders for an uninterrupted gaming experience.

02 / WORKFLOW

MY ROLE & WORKFLOW

As Lead Designer on this project (in collaboration with Black SMM agency), I spearheaded the visual direction from concept to delivery. I supervised on-site video/photo shoots, designed the Instagram feed and story architecture, created 2D animations and motion loops, and structured the template guidelines. The final concept was pitched as a dynamic video presentation.

03 / GUIDELINES

DESIGN SYSTEM & GUIDELINES

To maintain strong visual consistency across all digital touchpoints, the social media system strictly adhered to the core brand guidelines—balancing high-energy cyber aesthetics with functional, structured layouts.

Color Palette

The signature GOOD GAME palette thrives on the high-contrast clash of Acid Yellow and Ultraviolet, grounded by deep black and pure white. When choosing a primary background, black is always preferred. A neutral grey tone complements the palette, softening its visual aggression.

Print & Digital Execution: Standard laser printing cannot reproduce the vivid neon brightness of Acid Yellow and Ultraviolet; in such cases, monochrome graphics are recommended. For accurate color reproduction, offset printing, silkscreen, or PANTONE-certified inks are required. In physical spaces, the palette is brought to life through neon vinyl, UV lighting, and projectors.

Typography System

The centerpiece of the typographic identity is the Druk super-family, featuring a dynamic range of proportions from ultra-wide to extra-condensed. The most striking visual impact is achieved by pairing a wide font style on one line with a condensed style on the next. Druk is reserved strictly for large headlines.

For body text, captions, footnotes, and functional copy, the clean and highly legible Akzidenz-Grotesk typeface is used.

04 / SOCIAL MEDIA

FEED CONCEPT

Working closely with the SMM team, we established distinct visual templates for key content pillars. The 12-post grid combines graphic layouts, authentic on-site photography, and custom motion design - including animated looping graphics - to create a rhythmic, immersive feed.

05 / STORIES

MODULAR STORY BLOCKS

Stories were designed using a structured, block-based layout. This modular system maintains instant brand recognition while allowing fast, digestible storytelling for daily updates, price lists, and tournament promos.

06 / ADAPTATIONS

CAROUSELS & CROSS-FORMAT ADAPTATIONS

Info posts, tournament rules, and high-engagement giveaways were structured as multi-slide swipe carousels. Each visual asset was seamlessly adapted across Feed, Stories, and promo banners to maintain a unified campaign experience.
