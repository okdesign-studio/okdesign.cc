GoodGame Cyberlounge

EXPLORE FULL CASE
Client: GoodGame
Year: 2021
01 / The Challenge

Translating a neon-drenched cyber space into a structured digital identity.

GoodGame is a premium esports ecosystem and cyberlounge built for ultimate gaming immersion. Operating in collaboration with Black SMM, the primary objective was to architect a high-impact social media presence that reflected the physical space's futuristic, high-energy atmosphere.

The core challenge lay in developing a cohesive visual language across Instagram feeds and Stories — strictly adhering to established brand guidelines while pushing the boundaries of digital aesthetics through motion and typography.

02 / The Execution

Design Team Lead & Creative Direction.

Serving as the Design Team Lead and Lead Designer within the Black SMM framework, I directed the entire visual pipeline from concept design to final publishing. My role extended beyond static UI — I supervised on-site video and photo shoots, ensuring the content captured the raw, authentic energy of the gaming arena.

To provide complete transparency and visual clarity to the stakeholders, all feed concepts and content rubrics were pitched as dynamic video presentations (showcased in the hero video above), merging motion graphics with live-action footage.

03 / Visual System

High-contrast chromatic architecture.

The GoodGame palette is driven by the extreme contrast between Acid Yellow and Ultraviolet against deep Blackout backgrounds. White is strictly reserved for typography and noise accents, avoiding neutral greys to maintain an aggressive, futuristic tone.

While digital assets operate at maximum RGB saturation, physical signage and print materials rely on offset printing, silkscreen, and specialized UV-reactive neon vinyls to recreate the digital glow in the real world.

04 / Feed Architecture

A modular 12-post grid driven by seamless motion.

Working closely with the SMM strategy team, we established core visual rubrics to structure the brand narrative. To eliminate the monotony of standard static grids, I designed and animated seamless 2D/3D motion loops tailored specifically for Instagram.

The resulting 12-post ecosystem is a living, dynamic canvas where bold typography, on-site photography, and high-fps video loops coexist in perfect visual equilibrium.
