03 / Visual System

High-contrast chromatic architecture.

The core brand palette relies on a high-contrast collision of Acid Yellow and Ultraviolet, anchored by solid black and crisp white. When establishing visual hierarchy for backgrounds, black is always the preferred default. A neutral grey tone rounds out the spectrum, providing a stabilizing element to an otherwise aggressive palette.

Because standard laser printing cannot accurately reproduce the vibrant intensity of Acid Yellow and Ultraviolet, black-and-white graphics are recommended for conventional office use. To realize the brand colors in their true glory, physical production requires offset or silkscreen printing utilizing specific PANTONE inks, or premium materials like neon-yellow Oracal film and tinted paper stocks.

In physical environments, this aesthetic is brought to life using projectors, bespoke neon signage, and UV lighting.

All colors are strictly specified across PANTONE, CMYK, and RGB profiles.

04 / Typography

The Druk Superfamily.

The centerpiece of the GOOD GAME typography palette is the Druk superfamily. It combines typefaces of wildly different proportions — from ultra-wide to extremely condensed.

The most striking visual impact is achieved by pairing them—setting one line in an ultra-wide weight and the subsequent line in a condensed one. Druk is designed exclusively for large, bold headlines.

For body copy, captions, footnotes, and structural content, the classic Akzidenz-Grotesk typeface is utilized.

04 / Feed Architecture

A modular 12-post grid driven by seamless motion.

Working closely with the SMM strategy team, we established core visual rubrics to structure the brand narrative. To eliminate the monotony of standard static grids, I designed and animated seamless 2D/3D motion loops tailored specifically for Instagram.

The resulting 12-post ecosystem is a living, dynamic canvas where bold typography, on-site photography, and high-fps video loops coexist in perfect visual equilibrium.
