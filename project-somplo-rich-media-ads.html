Project Overview

My collaboration with Somplo extended beyond crafting UI/UX animations and promotional content for their core platform. I also designed and developed interactive Rich Media display ads for their global roster of clients.

The Approach

By merging my core design and animation skills with Somplo’s robust platform capabilities, I built premium, high-engagement ad experiences tailored to each brand's unique identity.
