Somplo Rich Media Ads

Client: Somplo
Year: 2025
Creative Execution: Oleksandr Kukharyk
EXPLORE FULL CASE
01 / OVERVIEW

THE CHALLENGE

Design and develop a suite of premium interactive Rich Media display ads for Somplo's global roster of clients — merging high-end visual identity with real-time interactive mechanics that push standard banners into engagement-driven digital experiences.

About the Project

My collaboration with Somplo extended beyond crafting UI/UX animations and promotional content for their core platform. I also designed and developed interactive Rich Media display ads for their global roster of clients, including Giorgio Armani, Nestle, Mazda, Lottusse, Toyota, Ralph Lauren, and L'Oréal.

Each ad was precision-crafted to mirror the client's brand identity and leverage Somplo's proprietary interactive formats — from 3D cube rotations and parallax effects to scratch overlays and multi-product carousels.

02 / WORKFLOW

MY ROLE & APPROACH

By merging my core design and animation skills with Somplo's robust platform capabilities, I built premium, high-engagement ad experiences tailored to each brand's unique identity — from creative concept and motion design to final interactive build and QA.

Design

Visual concept, brand adaptation, composition, colour grading, and motion storyboards for each format.

Animation

Frame-by-frame motion assets, CSS & JS animations, timeline coordination across multiple banner sizes.

Interactive Build

Rich Media ad production within the Somplo platform — RichBox, VideoBox, Multisize, and cross-format delivery.
